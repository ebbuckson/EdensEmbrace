About Us

Towards the end of 2021, I began my loc journey. I experienced watching my hair sprout and evolve after much trial and error. I documented my hair throughout each stage, conversed with other African-American women about our respective loc journeys, and researched techniques and myths regarding hair care. Eden's Embrace aims to destigmatize perceptions associated with dreadlocks and showcase the beauty of allowing our natural coils to take form.
